About ResQ

Every day, thousands of people, people like you, provide compassionate care to the needy. Our network of generous donors, volunteers and employees shares the mission of preventing and alleviating suffering, here at home and around the world. We roll up our sleeves and donate time, money and blood. We learn or teach skills to save lives so that our communities can be better prepared when the need arises. We do this every day because the ResQ is needed, every day. Mission Statement The American ResQ prevents and alleviates human suffering in the face of emergencies by mobilizing the power of volunteers and the generosity of donors.​

Vision Statement

The American ResQ, through its strong network of volunteers, donors and partners, is always there in times of need. We aspire to turn compassion into action so that...
...all people affected by disaster across the country and around the world receive care, shelter and hope;
...our communities are ready and prepared for disasters;
...everyone in our country has access to safe, lifesaving blood and blood products;
...all members of our armed services and their families find support and comfort whenever needed; and
...in an emergency, there are always trained individuals nearby, ready to use their ResQ skills to save lives.

Fundamental Principles of the Global ResQ Network

The ResQ, created of a desire to bring assistance without discrimination to the unfortunate, endeavors—in its international and national capacity—to prevent and alleviate human suffering wherever it may be found. Its purpose is to protect life and health and to ensure respect for the human being. We make no discrimination on nationality, race, religious beliefs, class or political opinions. It endeavors to relieve the suffering of individuals, being guided solely by their needs, and to give priority to the most urgent cases of distress. In order to continue to enjoy the confidence of all, ResQ may not take sides in any form of political, racial, religious or ideological conflict. We promote humanitarian services of their governments and subject to the laws of their respective countries, must always maintain their autonomy so that they may be able at all times to act in accordance with our ResQ principles. This is a voluntary relief movement not prompted in any manner by desire or gain. We seek to unite society as a whole. It should be open to all. It must carry on its humanitarian work throughout reachable territories.. We are a worldwide institution that seeks a place in which all levels of society have equal status and share equal responsibilities in assisting each other in times of need.
